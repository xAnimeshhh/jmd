Sale Formula

Total Sale = Cash Taken Home + Counter Cash Kept + GPay/Online + Counter Expenses − Yesterday's Counter Cash
Example: Took ₹2000 home, left ₹500 in counter, received ₹1000 GPay, spent ₹200 from counter, yesterday's counter was ₹800 →
Sale = 2000 + 500 + 1000 + 200 − 800 = ₹2900
